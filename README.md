# SoccerRobotArduino

# Description: 
Join us at Innovation Fest 2026 for a thrilling robot soccer competition, hosted by E-Council and Robobulls! Design, build, and program your bot to hold its ground and push the competition out of the ring. Build your way to victory within this heavy-hitting event! Whether you’re a seasoned pro or just starting, this is your chance to create, compete, and collaborate! Kits start at provided!

# Competition Rules
Robot Specifications
Maximum Dimensions: 15cm x 15cm x 15cm (fully assembled, prior to competition start).
Maximum Weight: 1500 grams.
Power Source: Must be battery-powered. No external power sources or tethers allowed.
Materials: All materials are permitted, but robots must not damage the ring surface or pose a safety hazard to other persons or their team created bot. Prohibited items include magnets, flywheels, or any mechanism designed for inflicting damage onto another bot.

# The Ring (Dohyo)
Dimensions: 60cm diameter circular ring.
Surface: Matte black finished wood or high-density plastic.
Border: A white border line marks the edge of the ring. A robot is out if any part of it touches the area outside the white line or the floor outside the ring.
Match Format
Duration: Each match will last a maximum of 90 seconds.

Scoring: A point is awarded when an opponent's robot is forced outside the white ring border.
Victory: The first robot to score two points wins the match. If time expires, the match is determined by the current score. If tied, a sudden-death overtime match of 30 seconds will be played.
Restart: If both robots become immobile or cease engagement for 10 consecutive seconds, the referee may restart the match.
